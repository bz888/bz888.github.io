updated 03/10/2021

Three new things I have learnt about myself and my ego as a result of the Core learning?

  • Learning new things is hard, I feel stupid and clueless, and that is normal, however in order for that to change is effort.
  • When learning more difficult concepts, my brain disconnects fast.
  • I am able to understand things, when I continuously tackle it.

What are the roles of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?

  • To understand who I am as a person and how I can improve in accordance with my learning speed.

What has surprised you the most about core learning?

  • The importance of mindset
  • In the coding industry, skill is not everything, there are other factors like EQ that all correlate to success in this field.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

  • Understanding my learning style and cater towards it

Why fixate so much on core learning in a web development bootcamp coourse?

  • Because, success in this industry does not 100% correlate to skill. Core learning is able to help with self development and enhancing the self. So when unpredictable problems arrive, the self is well-equipped to combat these problems.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead?
